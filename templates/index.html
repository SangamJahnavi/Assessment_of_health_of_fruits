ABOUT

Detecting rotten fruits has become significant in the agricultural industry.Separating rotten fruit is a necessary process that helps build trust and credibility before sending fresh products to consumers.This work proposed improving the model for predicting the rotten fruit that applied Convolutional Neural Network deep learning technique. The proposed model classifies the fresh fruits and rotten fruits and gives the percentage of health of fruit from the input fruit images. In this work, I have used three types of fruits, such as apple, banana, and oranges.The developed models has been built using the Convolutional Neural Network architecture with 13,599 images of fresh and rotten fruits for training, validating, and testing.

HOW TO USE

The architecture and design of the app has been thought with the objective to appear autonomous and simple to use. it is supposed to lead the user in the right direction with minimal interaction calls . The user needs to upload the image of the fruit and click on submit and then it predicts the health of the fruit.
